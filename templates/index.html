Welcome



Welcome to Therp-AI, Your Mental Health Assistant
Welcome to Therp-AI, your companion in your mental health journey. Our AI-powered bot is here to assist and support you as you navigate various aspects of mental well-being.


About Therp-AI
Therp-AI is a chatbot powered by advanced AI models designed to provide information, guidance, and support related to mental health topics. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, therapy, or diagnosis, but rather a tool to offer general insights, resources, and assistance.


Scope of Therp-AI's Assistance
Therp-AI is here to help answer your questions, offer guidance on coping strategies, provide information about mental health conditions, and direct you to relevant resources. It aims to provide a supportive environment for those seeking information about mental health.


Limitations
It's important to understand that while Therp-AI aims to be helpful and informative, it has limitations. For urgent, complex, or severe mental health concerns, it is crucial to seek assistance from qualified mental health professionals or emergency services.


Your Privacy Matters
We prioritize the privacy and confidentiality of your data. All information shared with Therp-AI is handled securely and treated with the utmost confidentiality. We do not share your data with third parties without your consent.


Get started
Feel free to start a conversation with Therp-AI and explore the resources and support it provides. Remember, for urgent or severe concerns, please seek immediate help from qualified professionals. Thank you for choosing Therp-AI as part of your mental health support system.
